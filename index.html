It's a jungle out there! The Belly Button hosts a fascinating collection of microbial diversity.
This page may be used as an educational resource to explore the different kinds of bacterial species that reside in the navel of the volunteers identified by their id numbers. Upon choosing a 'Test Subject Id No.' from the drop down menu, the corresponding charts are displayed. Hovering over the graphs will give the name of the microbe(s) associated with the OTU (Operational Taxonomic Unit) number.
Bacterial species identified here may be utilized in various application such as a flavor enhancer in the food industry.
